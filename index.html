INTENSITY ZONES

Z1 — Recovery, conversational pace, nose breathing comfortable.
Z2 — Aerobic endurance, can speak full sentences, 65–75% max HR. Most base miles live here.
Z3 — Tempo / sweet spot, 80–87% max HR, uncomfortable but sustainable 45–60min.
Z4 — Threshold intervals, 88–95% max HR, one-hour max effort.
Z5 — VO2max, all-out efforts of 1–5min.

KEY PRINCIPLES

80/20: ~80% of weekly volume should be easy (Z1–Z2), 20% hard (Z3+). Weak swimmers often flip this — don't.

Swim focus: Technique > volume. Drills every session. 2,000m continuous by week 18.

Bike focus: Your limiter by duration — ~55% of race time. Long ride non-negotiable.

Run focus: You have a base; maintain rather than push. Brick runs matter more than long runs.

Hard constraints baked in: CFA exam 23 May (taper week 5). Engineering exams 11–19 Jun (reduced volume). Peru 28 Jun–19 Jul (maintenance). Work full-time from late July (morning sessions + one long weekend ride).
